How This Started

Following recent shark incidents in NSW, a local ocean user contacted their local representative requesting a NSW shark deterrent rebate scheme similar to Western Australia's program.

The proposal was supported locally, referred to the local State MP, and has since been raised with the Minister for Environment for consideration.

The next step is to show that this idea has support from ocean users across NSW.

Why a Rebate?

  • Western Australia already provides rebates for approved shark deterrent devices.
  • These devices can cost hundreds of dollars.
  • NSW already invests in shark mitigation measures such as drones, tagging and public education.
  • A rebate would help make proven personal safety technology more accessible to ocean users.
